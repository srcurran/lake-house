The Dillingham-Curran Cabin

Lake George in the Adirondack Mountains

A cozy cabin with an open floor plan, large porch and panoramic lakefront views. Built in 1959 as a family retreat, it was recently remodeled with a new kitchen, bathrooms and fresh paint in 2015. This rustic getaway is complete with modern amenities including wireless internet and HD satellite TV. The house sleeps up to 8 (1 king, 2 queens, 2 twins) with bedrooms and bathrooms on both floors. The kitchen is great for entertaining with a cooking island, new appliances, dishwasher and a large dining table that seats up to 10. The house is dog friendly, so you can vacation with your furry friend-woof!

There are many opportunities to cook, read and get some sun. For those looking for outdoor activities, the lake offers great swimming, boating and fishing. For those who prefer to stay dry, there are nearby opportunities for tennis, golf and hiking. The house includes access to a diving board and a private dock that, while it is nearing the end of its service life, is great for swimming and can hold up to a 28‚Äù boat.

The small hamlet of Huletts Landing is a short drive or walk away, and offers several amenities including: boat launch, marina (which has a weekly farmers market, drinks, ice and Lake George gifts), ice cream parlor, post office, church and 9-hole golf course.

The cabin is 50 minutes north of Lake George Village; 20 minutes from the nearby town of Whitehall, NY, which includes several gas stations and pharmacies; 40 minutes to the nearest grocery in Fair Haven, VT; and 45 minutes south of Ticonderoga.
